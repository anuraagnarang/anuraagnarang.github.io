    		About Me

    I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Electronics at the Unviersity of Liverpool, having joined Dr Saqib Khursheed's reliability and security group in September 2018.

    My work is focused on methodologies that improve the dependability of integrated circuits (ICs), with the aim of enabling increased utilisation of ICs in safety-critical environments.
